We are going to space

Cardiff's first official mission to space is about to happen, and we are on the lookout for space cadets. Think you have what it takes?

The mission

The mission, should you accept, is to get Brian the Robot into space, film it, and get him back down safely and in one piece.

Over 4 sessions we will learn what is required to get into space, plan our mission, test launch various craft, and on the final launch day - we will get Brian into space!

The cadets

We are recruiting 20 Space Cadets between the ages of 7-14*.

F.H.S.A is intended for 7 - 14 year olds with an accompanying adult. Space Cadets will meet every Sunday afternoon at 2pm - 4pm on March 1st, 8th, 15th with a scheduled launch day of March 22nd. Mission control will be at FoundersHub in the middle of Cardiff city centre.

Apply now!
*Previous astronaut experience not required.
